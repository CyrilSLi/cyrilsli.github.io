Artist Bio

My name is Cyril Li, and I was born in 2009 in Shanghai, China. I started playing piano in 2014, and completed the RCM Level 4 practical exam in June 2019, Level 6 in June 2020, Level 8 in April 2021, and Level 9 in March 2022. I also completed the Level 8 theory exam in May 2021. I am currently studying Level 10 practical and Level 9 theory. I also played oboe for a few years and guitar at school. I like to listen to pop and classical music. I compose classical and experimental music. I have a lot of interest in computer science, specifically low level programming and circuit design.
